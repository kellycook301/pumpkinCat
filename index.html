Before Viewing the Pumpkin Cat

In the new area you can see the only Pumpkin Cat living in captivity. Please make sure to not look directly at him or you will experience extreme paranoia for days on end. Make sure to do ten push-ups before entering the exhibit. Pumpkin Cats appreciate push-ups and are less likely to inflict harm on those who take part in this ritual.
